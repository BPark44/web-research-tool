best cloud hosting for side projects

5/3/2026 · 4 products compared

For side projects, developers have shifted away from Heroku following Salesforce's 2026 decision to halt new feature development. The main contenders are DigitalOcean App Platform (a Heroku alternative built on the same buildpack model), AWS (full infrastructure control but steeper learning curve), and platforms like Northflank (which offer Heroku-like simplicity with your own cloud account). DigitalOcean stands out for side projects due to low entry cost, managed infrastructure, and 2-5x better compute pricing than Heroku.

Recommendation: For side projects, use DigitalOcean App Platform: it's the best Heroku replacement with $0-5/month entry cost, zero DevOps required, and transparent pricing. If your side project needs GPU-accelerated ML, Northflank offers simplicity with custom infrastructure. Avoid Heroku unless you're maintaining existing projects. Only choose AWS if your side project will scale significantly and you have DevOps expertise or time to learn.
